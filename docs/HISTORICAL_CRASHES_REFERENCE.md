# Historical Market Crashes Reference (1975-2025)

## Overview
This document provides verified historical data on 22 major market crashes from 1975-2025, based on objective criteria (>20% drawdown from peak).

## Crash Definition
A market crash is defined as a decline of **≥20% from peak to trough** in the S&P 500 index.
